The Natoan Conlang Project

The Natoan Conlang Project is an effort of mine to create a naturalistic conlang family for personal artistic purposes. "Natoan" is the term I use to refer to the project as a whole when speaking in English. The name "Natoan" is based on the current fictional endonym of the constructed proto-language for the Natoan project: Hanheme Natome Nawo (lit. "the forest people's words").
"Proto-Natoan" or "Archaic Natoan" is the name given to the conlang from which all members of the Natoan language family will be descended. Currently, Proto-Natoan is the only Natoan conlang in development.
